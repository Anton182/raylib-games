### Controls

Keyboard:

 - A-W-S-D -> Move the Roomba and clean the room.
 - SPACE -> Play Roomba sound.
 
Mouse:
 - Mouse move -> Control Cat movement, click on destination to go there.
 - Mouse right button -> Play Cat sound.
